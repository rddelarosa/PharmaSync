Our Story


At PharmaSync, our journey began with a simple mission: to ensure that no one ever misses a dose again.

Founded in 2025, we recognized the struggles individuals face in managing multiple prescriptions, remembering doses, and tracking refills. Our platform was created to simplify medication management by providing smart reminders, refill alerts, and a secure dashboard tailored to each user's needs.
